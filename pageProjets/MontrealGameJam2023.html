The Plague

  • GitHub: JolanThomassin/MontrealGameJam2023

Project Description

The creation of this game took place during the Montreal Game Jam 2023, organized by the Conjure club of ÉTS, in collaboration with Ubisoft. The goal of this event was to create a video game in 48 hours, in teams, based on a given theme (a historical event). This experience aimed to promote game creation, strengthen bonds among participants, foster mutual learning, encourage teamwork, and, of course, enjoy playing.

Take on the role of the magnificent Black Death in a medieval world; unfortunately for you, a terrible doctor continually resists your noble desire to conquer the land. To remedy this, you have various abilities to pursue him until his death. However, be careful—the doctor is cunning, possessing tricks up his sleeve to try desperately to save himself while protecting the peasants in his path.

Objective: Defeat the doctor before he creates a cure using the various life flowers scattered throughout the country.
